    • MIYA

    “An archer blessed by the Moon God.”

    Miya, born at the end of the Era of Strife, witnessed the elves' suffering during the Endless War and the corruption of her people by the Abyss. After the Moon Goddess created the Lunar Aegis, the Moon Elves flourished again. As the devoted Priestess of the Moon, Miya protected her people for millennia. Now, with the Abyss rising once more, she answers the ancient call to war, vowing under the moonlight to fight for her people.

    EUDORA

    “The master of the power of lightning.”

    Eudora is a Light Elf who possesses a high level of magical talent, yet always loses control of her emotions because of her being discriminated against due to her Elf status. Later, she was discovered by the mentor of the Magic Academy, Theresa, who took her to the Magic Academy to study. Finally, she overcame the gloom in her heart and succeeded Theresa, becoming the dean of the Ulturia School.

    LESLEY

    “Harley's guardian and a talented sniper.”

    After the death of her father, she was adopted by House Vance and became Harley's sister. Lesley regarded her younger brother as everything to her, and kept protecting Harley secretly.

    RAFAELA

    “The angel who heals everything.”

    Rafaela and her elder brother Argus are both angels, but Argus was treated differently because of his status as a male target. All praise was given to Rafaela, and all criticism was left to Argus. After a series of events that were left to ferment, rifts appeared in the relationship between the two siblings. Argus finally fell into the Abyss, but was helpless to do anything about. She is an angel whom people admire, but her elder brother continues to perish in pain.

    VEXANA

    “The young duchess guards the Necrokeep.”

    Vexana was the majestic ruler of Necrokeep, the gentle mother of the undead, but also the only glimmer of light that penetrated the Mist, bringing hope to this cursed land shunned by both life and death.

    Moon Blessing

    Each time Miya hits a target with her Basic Attack, she gains 5% Attack Speed for 4 seconds. Stacks up to 5 times.

    Moon Arrow

    Miya shoots two extra arrows with each Basic Attack, dealing 10–35 Physical Damage to the target enemy and 30% damage to nearby targets. This effect lasts 4 seconds.

    Miya launches an empowered arrow on the target area, dealing 270–420 Physical Damage to enemies within and immobilizing them for 1.2 seconds.

    Hidden Moonlight

    Miya removes all debuffs on her and conceals herself, gaining 35%–65% extra Movement Speed. This state lasts 2 seconds or until she launches an attack.
